Coding Paradigms

How should humans and AI actually write code together? Six working answers, each with its own tool, its own workflow, and its own view of where the human's attention belongs.

The debate. Is the AI an autocomplete (IDE-pair), a junior engineer you review (autonomous-agent), a compiler for specs (spec-driven), a team you manage (folder-as-agent, compound engineering), or a throwaway sketch pad (vibe coding)? Every answer implies a different skill set for the human.

IDE pair

Keep the human in the driver's seat; the AI suggests and completes inline. Cursor, Copilot, Zed.
