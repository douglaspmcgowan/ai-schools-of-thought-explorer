AI Economy & What Counts as a Product

If models keep getting cheaper and smarter, what's left to sell? Five schools answer differently — and the answer you pick tells you how to invest, what to build, and whether to believe your own roadmap.

The load-bearing argument. Sidu says most SaaS disappears as the capability line rises. Every says the best SaaS companies are quietly thriving. The bubble / not-bubble camp argue about whether the whole sector is mispriced. You can't hold all four positions.
