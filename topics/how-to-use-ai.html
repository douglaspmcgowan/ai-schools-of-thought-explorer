How to Use AI

The practical how-to layer. What experienced users have actually figured out — prompting craft, workflow recipes, clever tricks that turn raw capability into usable leverage.

Not the architecture debate. This topic sidesteps "what should the agent loop look like" and asks the smaller, more useful question: given what we have right now, how do you get the most out of it? Two schools — one on crafting prompts, one on crafting whole workflows.
