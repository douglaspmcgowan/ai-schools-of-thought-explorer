AGI Timelines

How fast are we moving, and how close is "it"? Four schools disagree — sometimes about the ceiling, sometimes about the road. They don't even agree on what "it" means.

The real split. Extrapolators trust the curves: scale + RL equals continuous progress toward human-level. Empiricists look at what models can actually do today and see gaps that scale alone won't close. LLM-paradigm doubters say the whole architecture is wrong. Post-AGI skeptics say the goal itself is a mirage.
