AI Schools of Thought

A living map of the competing ideas in AI right now — not the products, but the arguments. For each topic, pick a school, then read the people arguing for it. Three levels: topic → school → voice.

How to read this. Each topic is a live debate. Each school is a position inside that debate, named by what its adherents actually argue. Each voice is a specific essay, paper, or person — with the link, the date, and a one-line thesis so you can judge for yourself.
